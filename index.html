Enter Archmyst

Wraithlight is a multiplayer fantasy adventure set in a dangerous world shaped by Essence, ancient secrets, powerful technology, and the choices of its players.

Explore hostile regions, gather rare resources, master deep crafting systems, and uncover the truth behind the forces threatening the world.

Core Features

Multiplayer Exploration

Venture into a living world with other players, dangerous zones, and emergent encounters.

Deep Crafting

Use blueprints, rare materials, and mastery systems to create gear with meaningful identity.

Player Progression

Build your character through skills, specialties, and strategic choices rather than rigid classes.

A Reactive World

Your actions affect resources, instability, danger, and the balance of the world.
